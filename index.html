What Is Dark Country Music?

Dark country is a subgenre of American roots music that blends the storytelling of country with the darkness of gothic rock, death ballads, murder ballads, and outlaw blues. It's the music of midnight, graveyards, lost causes, and hard truths.

Gothic Americana

Where Southern Gothic literature meets country music. Haunted houses, lost love, and the shadow of the Old South.

Death & Murder Ballads

The oldest tradition in American folk — songs about death, violence, tragedy, and reckoning.

Outlaw Country

The rebellious spirit of Willie, Waylon, and Cash — amplified and pushed into darker territory.

Dark Blues

Where country meets the Delta blues at its most raw and unfiltered — the roots of American music laid bare.
