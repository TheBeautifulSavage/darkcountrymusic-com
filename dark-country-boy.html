About Dark Country Boy

Dark Country Boy is an independent American roots music artist operating at the outer edges of dark country, outlaw country, and gothic Americana. With 70 studio albums and 1,481 songs, the catalog is one of the most extensive in the genre — a body of work that documents the full range of dark country music's possibilities.

From swamp-gothic gator ballads to outlaw anthems, from death-blues meditations to Southern Gothic storytelling — Dark Country Boy's music exists in the tradition of Johnny Cash, Townes Van Zandt, and the murder ballad folk canon, while forging its own distinctly modern voice.

The music is available on all major streaming platforms and at the official site: darkcountryboy.net
